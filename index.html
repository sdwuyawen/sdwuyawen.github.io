Yawen Wu

Summary

I am Yawen Wu, a Senior Research Engineer at Qualcomm, starting in May 2023. I am building next-generation artificial intelligence (AI) for mobile devices. Prior to Qualcomm, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Notre Dame working with Dr. Yiyu Shi, starting in Spring 2023. Prior to Notre Dame, I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Pittsburgh in Feb. 2023 advised by Dr. Jingtong Hu. I am open to various kinds of collaboration, please drop me an email if you are interested in my research.

I received the MICCAI Young Scientist Award Nomination for my work on medical applications of on-device AI. My research internships at Meta and Baidu USA have resulted in technologies delivered to industrial production.

Research Interests

I aim to build efficient artificial intelligence (AI) for edge devices (on-device AI). My current research focuses on on-device machine learning (ML), aiming to build independent on-device ML including efficient on-device inference, on-device training, federated learning, fair on-device ML, and their medical applications. The outcomes of my research have been published at top-tier machine learning, EDA, embedded system, and medical application venues.

In particular, my current interests are as follows:

  1. Efficient machine learning on edge devices.
  2. Federated learning.
  3. Self-supervised learning.
  4. Fairness in machine learning.
