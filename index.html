Yawen Wu

Summary

Yawen Wu is currently a fifth year Ph.D candidate in machine learning at the Department of Computer Engineering, the University of Pittsburgh, advised by Dr. Jingtong Hu and has close collaboration with Dr. Yiyu Shi.

He is currently working on algorithm design and optimization to improve the efficiency and performance of machine learning algorithms and systems. My research interests are efficient machine learning and computer vision on edge devices, federated learning, and self-supervised learning. I have published 10 papers as the first author on these topics, generating more than 160 citations.

Research Interests

  • On-device machine learning.
  • Federated learning.
  • Self-supervised learning.
  • Medical applications of machine learning.
