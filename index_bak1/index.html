Yawen Wu

Short Bio

Yawen Wu is currently a third year Ph.D student in Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh, advised by Dr. Jingtong Hu. He received the B.E. and M.S. degrees from the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University, China, in 2013 and 2016, respectively.

Research Interests

  • On-Device Artificial Intelligence
  • Intermittent Computing and Communication
